REALITY ARENA

Conreality is a tech startup building products & services for shooting live-action games like airsoft and paintball. We create hardware & software for arena operators, players & players teams.

SOFTWARE

We create mobile-first software that helps players and teams communicate, coordinate and plan better their game strategy & tactics. This year we're introducing support for augmented reality wearables for a unique, never before seen live-action game experience.

MKI DRONE GUNSHIP CONCEPT

Build around the proven DJI Matrice 600, this concept drone gunship is manned by a crew of two. The payload is outfitted with our own design of gimballed paintball turret and carrying capacity of several hundred paintballs.

MK2 SENTRY TURRET PROTOTYPE

The turret comes in several form factors, materials, and payload options. It features automatic trigger control and object recognition enabling your team to cover crucial choke points on the field or during CQB. Weyland-Yutani Corporation approved.

QUICK FACTS

Conreality was started during a September 2015 hackathon taking place in a friend's kitchen in Bratislava, Slovakia as an open-source robotics project for live-action games. After several years of conceptioning and R&D process we launched the company in 2019 and raised the first angel investment. In 2020 we opened the first showroom in Lviv, Ukraine displaying the manufactured prototypes & products.
In 2021 we're launching our unique live-action games platform to the wide audience.
